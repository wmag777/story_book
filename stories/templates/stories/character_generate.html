Prompt Writing Tips

For best results, include these details in your generation prompt:

  • Age and gender: "12-year-old boy", "elderly woman", etc.
  • Physical features: Hair color/style, eye color, skin tone, height/build
  • Clothing: Specific outfit details, colors, accessories
  • Expression/mood: "friendly smile", "determined look", etc.
  • Pose: "standing confidently", "sitting cross-legged", etc.
  • Distinctive features: Glasses, freckles, scars, tattoos, etc.
Note: The more specific your prompt, the better consistency you'll achieve across scenes.
